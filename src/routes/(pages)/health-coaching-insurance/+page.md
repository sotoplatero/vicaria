## Get Coached to Better Health: Comparing Insurance Plans with Health Coaching in Canada

Health insurance is no longer just about covering unexpected medical bills. Leading Canadian insurers are now integrating proactive **Health Coaching** services to help clients manage chronic conditions, improve their nutrition, and boost their overall wellness.

For residents of Ontario and beyond, choosing the right plan means looking beyond the basics to see what personalized support is available. This article compares five prominent options active in Ontario, highlighting who they cover, what coaching they offer, and how you access the service.

2.  **Manulife Vitality**
3.  **Canada Life (in partnership with Shoppers Drug Mart)**
4.  **Green Shield Canada (GSC)**

### Blue Cross: Comprehensive Wellness for Individuals and Groups

Blue Cross (including Ontario Blue Cross for individuals and Medavie Blue Cross for groups) is a strong contender for those seeking broad wellness support.

* **Coaching Focus:** Blue Cross offers personalized coaching for specific chronic conditions like diabetes (Type 1 and 2), heart health (hypertension, weight control), lung health (COPD, smoking cessation), and even menopause health.
* **Unique Feature:** All Blue Cross members gain access to the **inConfidenceÂ® program**, which provides 24/7 holistic support from health professionals like registered nurses, dietitians, life coaches, and legal/financial advisors.
* **Access & Cost:** Services are available via a confidential telephone line, videoconference, or online platform. Health coaching programs are included in the insurance premium at **no direct additional cost** for policyholders.

[Learn More: Personalized Health Coaching & Care | Medavie Blue Cross](https://www.medaviebc.ca/en/members/your-coverage/health-coaching)

### Manulife Vitality: The Fitness App That Pays You Back

Manulife offers the **Manulife Vitality** program as an interactive wellness complement to its individual life and health policies. This program is especially suited for active individuals who are digitally savvy.

* **Coaching Focus:** Vitality gamifies healthy habits, rewarding users for physical activity, good nutrition (via a connected app like Cronometer), mental health support, and preventive screenings.
* **Unique Feature:** You can earn discounts on your insurance premiums by maintaining a high level of Vitality points, effectively rewarding healthy behavior. The premium **Vitality Plus** tier offers advanced services like virtual pediatric care sessions (KixCare) and personalized pharmacogenetic testing.
* **Access & Cost:** The service is centered around the Manulife Vitality mobile app. All "coaching" occurs virtually through the digital platform and external partners. The basic Vitality program is often included with an eligible policy, meaning there is **no visible monthly fee** for the user.

[Learn More: Manulife Vitality Expands with New Partners and Personalized Health Tools](https://www.manulife.com/ca/en/about-us/news/manulife-vitality-expands-with-new-partners-and-personalized-hea)

### Canada Life & Shoppers Drug Mart: Team Coaching for Chronic Conditions

Canada Life, a major provider of employee benefits, offers a robust health coaching program through a partnership with Health Solutions by Shoppers Drug Mart. This applies to group insurance plans.

* **Coaching Focus:** This is specialized team coaching for employees with key chronic conditions that impact the workplace: specifically **diabetes, cardiovascular disease, and obesity**.
* **Unique Feature:** The coaching is provided by a multidisciplinary team: a registered nurse acts as the primary coach, coordinating with pharmacists (for medication management) and dietitians/nutritionists (for nutritional advice). No prescription or medical referral is required.
* **Access & Cost:** The model is virtual-first, with sessions delivered via phone or video conference. For the employee, the service is offered at **no direct cost (no copay)**, as it's provided as part of the health benefits.

[Learn More: Shoppers Drug Mart announces health coaching program for employers | Wealth Professional](https://www.wealthprofessional.ca/investments/life-and-health-insurance/shoppers-drug-mart-announces-health-coaching-program-for-employers/256677)

### Green Shield Canada (GSC): Specialized Nutritional Coaching

Green Shield Canada (GSC) incorporates a dedicated **Dietitian Health Coaching Program** into its group plans.

* **Coaching Focus:** The core focus is nutritional coaching for weight control and the prevention/management of diet-related chronic diseases (e.g., diabetes, hypertension).
* **Unique Feature:** The program is delivered in alliance with Loblaws supermarkets, allowing the insured to choose between **in-store, face-to-face sessions** with a Loblaws dietitian or a virtual experience. The program consists of four sessions over 4 to 8 months.
* **Access & Cost:** Members use GSC's Change4Life portal to enroll and find a dietitian. The total program cost is estimated at **\$275 CAD** (as of 2023), which is usually fully covered by the employer's plan.

[Learn More: GSC Update - January 2023 | GreenShield](https://www.greenshield.ca/en-ca/our-stories/gsc-update-january-2023)

### Desjardins Insurance & Constant Health: Digital Support for Obesity Treatment

Desjardins has partnered with the digital health company Constant Health to offer a specialized program for employees with clinical obesity or medical weight management needs.

* **Coaching Focus:** This program provides comprehensive support for individuals using or starting **obesity medications** (like GLP-1 therapies), combining intensive one-on-one coaching (over 5 hours) with behavior modification.
* **Unique Feature:** The 15-week program uses the Constant Health mobile app for daily tracking, data analysis, and secure messaging with a registered dietitian coach trained in cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT).
* **Access & Cost:** The entire program is virtual. The list price is approximately **\$750 CAD** for the full 15-week cycle. However, Desjardins plans typically cover at least \$500, making the effective cost for the participant low or even zero, depending on their exact coverage.

[Learn More: Obesity Medication Support | Constant Health & Desjardins](https://www.constanthealth.ca/desjardins/aom)

### How to Choose the Right Health Coaching Plan

Selecting the best plan depends on your individual needs or the needs of your employee group. Use these steps to guide your decision:

1.  **Assess Your Needs:** Are you looking for general wellness and fitness rewards (like Manulife Vitality), or do you need specialized support for a chronic condition (like the targeted programs offered by Canada Life or Desjardins)?
2.  **Check Plan Type:** Verify if the service is available through your individual policy or if it is only offered as part of your employer's group benefits package. Group benefits often offer more intensive, targeted coaching at a lower cost to the employee.
3.  **Confirm Access Method:** Decide if you prefer virtual support (most common), which offers flexibility, or if in-person coaching (like the GSC/Loblaws option) is essential for you.
4.  **Review the Cost Structure:** While most coaching services are covered, check for any potential deductibles, copays, or out-of-pocket expenses, especially for specialized treatments.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) about Insurance with Health Coaching in Ontario

### 1. Is Health Coaching available on individual insurance or only on group (employer) plans?

Health Coaching is available in both types. Individual plans often include broad wellness programs, while group plans tend to offer more specialized, targeted coaching for chronic diseases.

### 2. Do I have to pay an additional cost (copay or fee) for Health Coaching if my insurance includes it?

In most cases, the service is free or heavily subsidized for the final insured member, as the cost is typically included in the overall policy premium paid by the individual or the employer.

### 3. Does coaching focus on specific health problems or general wellness?

It depends on the insurer. Individual plans often cover general wellness (e.g., Manulife Vitality), while group plans often target chronic conditions (e.g., diabetes, obesity) that impact employee health.

### 4. Is the Manulife Vitality program a complete health insurance plan?

No, Manulife Vitality is not an insurance plan itself. It is an interactive wellness program offered as a rider (complement) to certain eligible life and health insurance policies from Manulife.

### 5. Do I need a doctor's referral (prescription) to access these coaching programs?

Generally, no. Most programs allow direct enrollment. However, highly specialized programs, such as those supporting obesity medication, are intended for individuals with a clinical medical indication for that condition.

### 6. Who offers in-person coaching sessions in Ontario?

**Green Shield Canada (GSC)** offers the option for **in-person** sessions with registered dietitians at certain participating Loblaws stores. Most other programs operate entirely in a **virtual** mode (phone or video call).

### 7. Can dependent children also use these coaching services?

Yes, in many cases, dependents are covered, with some age restrictions.

* The **GSC Dietitian Health Coaching** is available to members and dependents **aged 16 and over**.
* **Blue Cross** individual plans cover the policyholder and included dependents.
* **Manulife Vitality Plus** includes benefits such as virtual pediatric care (KixCare) for minors.
